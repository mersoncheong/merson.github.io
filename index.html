ABOUT ME

I am currently heading into my Final Year of my Bachelor Degree (Data Science & Analytics) at the National University of Singapore.

I have always been a lazy person growing up. I loved finding the easiest yet most effective ways to solve my problems at hand. This was as simple as experimenting with multiple transport routes to school so I maximize the amount of sleep I was getting everyday or calculating the perfect combination of canteen food I could purchase with my allowances.

During my pre-tertiary days, my goal was to pursue a degree in Business Administration, as I believed it aligned with my innate passion for collaborating with people to solve real-world problems. However, this had a deviation during my first internship during late 2020 at a local SME. Here, I was mainly tasked to provide the company with analyis against the bigger market players. During this,I found myself gravitating to providing numbers to support my analysis and I eventually stumbled into the world of Data Analytics. I was instantly intrigued by the potential of this field and was frustrated that I lacked the technical skills to implement the concepts within. I took it upon myself afterwards to learn more about this field through a variety of online courses and made the decision to switch my major right before matriculating in 2021.

I intend to use this website to write about my experiences outside of what is seen on the CV.
